# Samsung Tablet - Clear Chrome Cache

## Simple Steps:

1. **Open Chrome** on the tablet

2. **Tap the 3 dots** in the top right corner (⋮)

3. **Tap "Settings"**

4. **Tap "Privacy and security"**

5. **Tap "Clear browsing data"**

6. **Select these items:**
   - ✅ Cached images and files
   - ✅ Site settings (optional but recommended)

   **Do NOT select:**
   - ❌ Browsing history
   - ❌ Cookies and site data (this will log you out)

7. **Choose "All time"** from the time range dropdown

8. **Tap "Clear data"**

9. **Close Chrome completely** (swipe up from bottom and swipe Chrome away)

10. **Reopen Chrome** and try uploading again

---

## Alternative Method (Faster):

1. **Long press** the Chrome icon
2. Tap the **"i" (App info)** icon
3. Tap **"Storage"**
4. Tap **"Clear cache"** (NOT "Clear data")
5. Done!

---

## What to Try:

After clearing cache, go to the website and:
1. **Click "View Debug Log"** button at the top
2. Try uploading **one small photo** first
3. If it fails, click **"Copy Log"** button
4. Send me the log text

This will show exactly where the tablet is running out of memory.
